Pipeline & Phone

Cooling

The pipeline cooled on paper — no new Hot leads or reservations this period, warm count at 24 (down from 31) — but the bench quality is the best it's been: multiple $4M–$6M prospects are in active dialogue, including a NY relocator shopping the $4–5M range, a returning ~$6M prospect on his fourth visit cycle, and an LA couple who registered saying they're "super interested to secure ours now." Sales visits held steady at 15. The phone is the real worry: 8 of 21 calls went unanswered (38%), dropping the answer rate from 87% to 76% — and half of all calls now come from the print ad number.
