Key Recommendations

  1. 1 High Fix the missed call rate. 8 of 27 calls (30%) went unanswered. At $190 per lead, that's roughly $1,520 in ad spend that didn't get a human response. Add a call routing backup or after-hours voicemail-to-text system.
  2. 2 High Cut wasteful campaigns. The Google Competitor campaign spent $1,888 for one lead, and the Feeder Market campaign runs at $777/lead. Pause or significantly reduce these and reallocate to Branded Search and Meta Prospecting.
  3. 3 Medium Improve landing page conversion. Google Ads has a strong 10.1% CTR but only 1.14% of clicks convert. Even a modest lift to 2% would nearly double leads without increasing spend. Test clearer CTAs, shorter forms, or dedicated landing pages for top campaigns.
  4. 4 Medium Fix the homepage bounce rate. At 65%, the homepage is losing two-thirds of visitors immediately. Interior pages (Residences 9%, Amenities 11%) perform well — consider sending ad traffic directly to these pages, or redesign the homepage with stronger above-the-fold content and clear next steps.
  5. 5 Opportunity Increase Meta budget. Meta delivers leads at $172 vs Google's $282 and is uniquely effective at reaching real estate agents/brokers from Miami and South Florida. Shifting even $2K from underperforming Google campaigns to Meta could yield 10+ additional leads.
